# Privacy Policy — My Zen Master

**Last updated:** April 2, 2026

## Overview

My Zen Master is an offline Buddhist reflection app. Your privacy is fundamental to its design — everything runs on your device.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.** Period.

- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No user accounts
- No server-side processing

## On-Device Data

- **Conversation history** is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.
- **Personal memories** (if enabled) are stored in a local database on your device. You can clear them at any time from the Settings screen.
- **AI model** — A language model (~1.8 GB) is downloaded once from a secure CDN (Cloudflare R2) to your device. After download, all AI processing happens entirely offline.

## Network Access

The app connects to the internet **only** to download the AI model on first launch. After that, no network access is required or used.

## Third-Party Services

- **Cloudflare R2** — Used solely to host the AI model file for initial download. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to that download: [https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

No other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks are used.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
