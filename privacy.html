Privacy Policy

Effective: April 1, 2026

The short version: My Zen Master runs entirely on your device. We don't collect your data. We don't track you. We don't even have a server to store anything on. Your reflections are yours alone.

What We Collect

Nothing. Zero. My Zen Master does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. There are no analytics, no crash reporters, no usage tracking, and no telemetry of any kind.

How the App Works

My Zen Master is a Buddhist reflection and insight companion powered by a large language model (LLM) that runs entirely on your device. Your conversations, reflections, and insights never leave your phone. There is no cloud processing — every response is generated locally, in the palm of your hand.

Network Activity

The app makes a single network request when you first set it up: downloading the language model from a content delivery network (CDN). After that initial download, the app functions completely offline. No data is sent anywhere, ever.

No Accounts, No Sign-Ups

There are no user accounts, no email collection, no sign-in flows. You open the app and begin. That's it.

No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No SDKs watching what you do. No ad networks. No social media trackers.

Your Data, Your Device

Any data the app creates — conversation history, preferences, saved reflections — lives solely on your device in the app's sandboxed storage. If you delete the app, that data is gone. We have no copy of it, because we never had access to it in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data from anyone, we also collect no data from children. The app is designed for general audiences seeking mindful reflection.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page and the effective date above. But our core commitment is simple and unlikely to change: your data stays on your device.
