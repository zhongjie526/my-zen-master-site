DharmaMonk — Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How big is the download?

The app itself is small. On first launch, it downloads an AI model (~1.8 GB) from a CDN. After that, everything runs offline — no further downloads needed.

Why does it need so much storage?

The AI model runs entirely on your device so your conversations stay private. That requires a larger model file, but it means nothing is ever sent to a server.

Can I use it without internet?

Yes — after the initial model download, the app works completely offline. Airplane mode, no Wi-Fi, anywhere.

Does it collect any data?

No. Zero analytics, zero tracking, zero cloud. Your reflections never leave your phone. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

What devices are supported?

DharmaMonk requires an iPhone with at least 4 GB of RAM (iPhone XS or later) running iOS 16+.

The responses feel slow — is that normal?

The AI runs on your phone's processor, so responses take 15–30 seconds depending on your device. Newer iPhones are faster. This is the trade-off for complete privacy.

Can I clear my conversation history?

Yes — tap the new conversation button (pencil icon) in the top right to start fresh. The master's memory of your past reflections can be cleared in Settings.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. DharmaMonk is a reflection companion, not a therapist. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please reach out to a qualified professional or your local emergency services.
